# UIUC EE Course Selection & Archive

This repository documents my academic journey through the Electrical Engineering curriculum at the **University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign**. I want to share with you ECE courses experience and genearal education suggestion while wrapping up my last semester. I am used to take notes with pen and paper, so I might not upload them, unless asked or have so much boring time.
  
The ECE course system at UIUC covers almost everything, I have took courses in almost every area of interest.
